Information Theory

Oct 14 2016

This talk, delivered by Sidhant Saraogi was held on Friday October 14, 2016 in MC 5479.

Abstract

I will try to provide a brief introduction to Information Theory working towards motivating Shannon's Source Coding Theorem. We will use rather simple examples (for e.g. Repition Codes) to explain the idea behind the theorem and eventually try to prove it for a rather specific example.
